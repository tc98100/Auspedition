ENJOY FOOD IN CENTENNIAL HOMESTEAD

A Dining Room In A Glass House Filled With Wisteria

By Bonnie Wu

Introduction

There are so many great parks in Sydney, but I especially like Centennial Park, where you can ride bicycles, see black swans, summer water and horseback riding, as well as the restaurant Centennial Homestead. Centennial Homestead is divided into two parts, one side is the main restaurant with coffee and wine list, the other side is a small coffee shop where you can take away your coffee if you don't want to sit down and stroll around the park. Or take a photo with the wisteria.

Centennial Homestead’s Kitchen

Centennial Homestead’s Kitchen is open for breakfast and lunch Monday to Sunday from 8am – 3pm. Walk-ins are welcome but to avoid disappointment, please make a booking below.

Takeaway

The Pantry is open for all your takeaway needs in the heart of the Centennial Parklands Monday to Sunday from 8am – 4pm. Wherever you’ve come from, consider our home yours at Centennial Homestead.
